The project

This is a personal project I have been working on for the last 2 and a half years. It is a lifestyle, photography, travel and food blog. It is a place in which I explore writing, photography and hand lettering. I also spent time branding the blog, creating a logo, clear visual theme and choosing layouts.

The blog began with an Instagram account on which I posted hand lettering, and prints. It has evolved to become something more varied. I enjoy spending time drawing, painting, creating, taking photos and curating the feed. Scrolling back through the Instagram provides a snapshot of the evolution of EM Designs.

Tools used - Photoshop, Lightroom, Cannon 600d
