About

Promotional Material created for the Massey Association of Communications Students (MACS). These motion graphics were created to get people interested in becoming a member of the MACS executive. MACS is a club that supports students studying Communications at Massey University. They organise events throughout the year connecting students with industry and providing social support and skills workshops.

Tools used - Adobe Illustrator & After effects
